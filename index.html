Voice AI Receptionist

stack Vapi · Google Calendar · Airtable
Who is this for

Small businesses, agencies, and solo professionals who want to automate appointment scheduling and caller follow-up through a voice-based AI receptionist. If you're using tools like Google Calendar, Airtable, and Vapi, this setup is for you.

The problem it solves

Manual call handling, appointment booking, and email coordination is time-consuming and error-prone. This workflow automates the entire receptionist role: answering calls, checking calendar availability, managing appointments, and storing call summaries, all without human intervention.

How it works

Real-Time Job Scraper

stack Camoufox · Playwright · Anti-Bot Evasion
Who is this for

Recruiters, job boards, market intelligence teams, and businesses that need to monitor competitor hiring or aggregate job listings at scale, without getting blocked.

The problem it solves

Job boards and ATS platforms aggressively block automated scraping. Traditional scrapers get IP-banned within minutes. This system uses the Camoufox browser, an anti-fingerprint browser, combined with Playwright for stealth browsing, rotating Google dork queries across 7 major ATS platforms (Greenhouse, Lever, Workable, BambooHR, Ashby, SmartRecruiters, Jobvite), and intelligent consent handling to bypass anti-bot detection and extract 1,000+ fresh job postings per week.

How it works

The scraper runs headless Camoufox, which spoofs browser fingerprints to evade detection. It cycles through Google dork queries targeting specific ATS platforms, paginates up to 3 pages per source, handles cookie consent popups, and extracts job titles, company names, locations, and descriptions. Duplicate detection prevents re-scraping. Results are saved as structured JSON and served via a live Vercel-hosted job board.

Google Review Automation

stack n8n (No-Code) · Google Business Profile · OpenAI
Who is this for

Local businesses, service providers, and agencies who need to actively manage their Google reviews, respond fast, track sentiment, and keep their online reputation healthy without checking Google manually every day.

The problem it solves

Google reviews directly impact local SEO and customer trust, but most businesses either ignore them or respond late. A single negative review left unanswered can cost dozens of leads. Manually monitoring reviews, drafting responses, and notifying the right team members is tedious and easy to drop. This n8n workflow automates the entire pipeline.

How it works

Triggered by a new Google Business Profile review, the workflow formats the review data and sends it to OpenAI for an intelligent, brand-aligned response. It then classifies the review as positive or negative. Negative reviews trigger an urgent Slack alert to the team so they can act fast. Positive reviews notify the team for engagement. Every review, AI response, and rating is logged to Google Sheets with timestamps, review IDs, and full context for easy tracking.
